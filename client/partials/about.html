ORACLE CORPORATION

2013 - 2015

Because of his involvement in the Sales Engineering Club, Andy launched his career as a Solutions Engineer where he used problem solving skills to uncover potential client bottlenecks, prepare POCs with front-end development, and lead technical product demonstrations of HR SaaS solutions to a variety of HR Leaders and Executives. ZENEFITS

2015 - 2016

After working for 2 years as a Solutions Engineer, Andy decided to try his hand at Operations and dive into the world of startups. He also continued building front-end applications to support the Operations Team in streamlining its internal processes.

CODING DOJO SAN JOSE

2016 - 2017

Zenefits had begun to rebrand itself and was rebuilding itself from the ground up. IMAJINE STUDIO

Andy's first Software Engineering gig was with Imajine Studio, a small digital marketing company based in the greater DC Area. UPGUARD

2017 - 2017

After doing contract work with Imajine Studio, Andy worked as a Global Support Engineer (GSE) at UpGuard, a company focused on providing Cyber Resilience to SMB and Enterprise IT organizations. Through all this, he gained exposure to IT architecture, Virtual Machines, and the fundamentals of cyber security concerns.

Betterworks

2018 - PRESENT

After a large internal change at UpGuard, Andy returned to do Solutions Engineering work at betterworks, a growing company focused on OKRs (Objectives and Key Results) and CPM (Continuous Performance Management) methodologies. Betterworks strives to take the processes and methodologies made famous by John Doerr (Google + "Measure What Matters") and give growing startups and enterprises the same opportunity to scale and grow just like the other tech giants (ie Google & Intel) have. As a Solutions Engineer, Andy serves as a Subject Matter Expert on the betterworks platform while serving as a project manager/consultant to both internal and external stakeholders. The role requires a combination of both technical know-how as well as business acumen in order to effectively demonstrate the value proposition of the company's product. Andy continues to grow and develop both of these skill sets through his current role as an SE.
